Welcome to List Central!

List Central was the creation of Brahmina Burgess, that's me! I wanted to make a cool social network that would both be fun, and orderly, thus conquering the world with lists. Well, it didn't really turn out that way for List Central, and I lost my momentum for this site. I learned many things through the process, including how vital a business model is! I have since moved on... but building List Central from the ground up helped my gain a ton of technical skills and experience.

I will continue to leave List Central up in it's current state, as I love it, and have many bits and pieces saved in list form under my account. If you like List Central, keep using it! I apologyze for the lack of progress and maintenence that will go into List Central going forward. To learn more about the vision that was List Central, be sure to stop by the about page.

If you'd llike to check out what I'm up to now... check out my main site: Brahmina Creations, and my blog: Brahmina Writes.
