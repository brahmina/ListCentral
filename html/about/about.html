About List Central

The spot online where lists go to play.

List Central is a unique publishing platform -- somewhere between blogging, and micro-blogging -- to be known as list-blogging. By combining the inherent usefullness of lists, with online social rankings, link & image sharing & content ownership, List Central is the ultimate social community for list-lovers everywhere!

By providing the option to either keep lists private, or publish them, List Central gives its users the best of both worlds: usefulness, creativity & fun. List Central users can create both practical lists to help get organized, as well as creative fun lists to share with the online community. Users can rate each others public lists in the site wide competition to get their lists among the highest ranked on the front page.
