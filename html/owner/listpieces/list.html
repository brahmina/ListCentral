Edit list

List Central features edit in place functionality for list items, that do not have links associated with them, and list item descriptions. Click on any of your list's items and item descriptions to edit.

When you have finished with your edits, you can save them by clicking the save button(), or by hitting the Ctrl+Alt+S keys together. You can cancel your edits by either clicking the cancel button (), or by hitting the escape key (Esc).

If the list item has an associated link, you can still edit the list item by clicking on the edit button() to the left of the list item. This will display a form that enables you to edit any aspect of that particular list item.
