Edit in Place

List Central features edit in place functionality for editing your list groups and status sets.

Click on the list group or status set you wish to edit. When you have finished with your edits, you can save them by clicking the save button(), or by hitting the Ctrl+Alt+S keys together. You can cancel your edits by either clicking the cancel button (), or by hitting the escape key (Esc).
